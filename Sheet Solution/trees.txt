Trees contribute significantly to a rich, healthy ecosystem. Animals, insects, birds, and fungi find their homes in the trees, creating a diverse and vibrant ecosystem. This balanced environment, in turn, contributes significantly to the betterment of human beings. Trees produce their own food and are found at the very bottom of the food chain. How do trees accomplish this feat? Through photosynthesis, photosynthesis, the remarkable process that harnesses sunlight to create nourishment for the tree and, subsequently, the entire ecosystem.
